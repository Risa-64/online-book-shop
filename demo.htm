Are polls and pollsters biased?

I don't give a hoot who you are, what you are, what your politics are, how rich or poor you are, or where you live. I want to hear from you.

Have you ever been contacted by a pollster? Probably not. I'm 80 and no one has ever contacted me to ask my opinion of anything.

Most "scientific" polls are produced with a "sample" of approximately 1,000 poll respondents and they rarely segment the sample. The results in 2016 were devastating for pollsters. Only one pollster called it for Donald Trump in the days leading up to the election.
